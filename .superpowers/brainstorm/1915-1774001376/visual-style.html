Visual Direction: Assembly Builder

Which visual style captures the right feel for the cross-section builder?

A: Pure Technical Drawing

Monospace fonts, dimension lines, hatching patterns per material standard. Feels like a CAD section. Grid paper background. Monochrome with subtle material tones.

B: Bold Dark Mode

Dark background, color-coded layers with glowing borders, large typography for GWP numbers. Hatches overlaid on colored fills. Modern data-viz energy. Eye-catching.

C: Technical + Portfolio Style

White background with bold 2px borders and offset shadows from your PortfolioPage style. Technical hatches per material, but with your pink/accent color palette. GWP per layer + total. Best of both worlds.
